Vision: Position Amaravati as India's Quantum Gateway and globally recognized hub for research, innovation, and applications.

2. QUANTUM COMPUTING FUNDAMENTALS
‚ãÆ

Quantum Computing leverages quantum bits (qubits) with properties like superposition, entanglement, and quantum interference for exponential computational speedups.

  • Superposition: Qubits exist in multiple states simultaneously
  • Entanglement: Correlated qubits enable parallel computations
  • Key Algorithms: Shor's algorithm (factoring), Grover's algorithm (search)
  • Current Stage: NISQ (Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum)

Applications Ready for Deployment: Drug discovery, material science, optimization, cryptography.

3. GLOBAL QUANTUM LANDSCAPE & STRATEGIC APPLICATIONS
‚ãÆ

Over 30 countries committed USD 40B+ in public investments. Leading quantum application areas:

  • Cryptography & Cybersecurity: QKD and post-quantum cryptography standards
  • AI & Machine Learning: VQE, QAOA for accelerated optimization
  • Healthcare & Pharma: IBM-Moderna RNA folding simulations for vaccine design
  • Materials Science: Quantum simulations for superconductors, catalysts, batteries
  • Energy & Climate: Grid simulation for renewable optimization
  • Sensing & Metrology: Atomic clocks, gravimeters, magnetometers reaching commercial maturity
